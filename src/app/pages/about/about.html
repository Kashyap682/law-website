About

Parthiv Joshi is value-driven advocate enrolled with the Bar Council of Gujarat, practicing out of Rajkot. He has graduated in B.A. LL.B. (Hons.), a five year integrated law course, from the reputed Gujarat National Law University, Gandhinagar. His experience, through his internships, spans both sides of the tax landscape – the taxpayer and the tax administrator – equipping him with skills to tailor balanced and practical solutions.

He has engaged at every level of the Indian judicial system, from trial and adjudicatory forums to appellate and constitutional courts. Further, he has valuable gained insights into tax administration through field interactions and an internship at the Central Board of Indirect Taxes, New Delhi. His key areas of interest include constitutional law, indirect taxation, and commercial law.

Driven by a commitment to effective and fair representation for all, he brings a fresh energy and a principled approach to his practice.

Practice Focus

Original and appellate advocacy before various courts, tribunals, forums, and authorities in Gujarat.
